Nand Vatika Internship Report
This repository contains the internship report for a four-week civil engineering training program completed at Nand Vatika Developers Pvt. Ltd., Ranchi as part of the B.Tech in Civil & Environmental Engineering curriculum at Birla Institute of Technology, Mesra.​

Overview
The internship focused on gaining practical exposure to real estate construction projects, with emphasis on planning, execution, and site management for multi-storey residential buildings under the project “Nand Vatika Greens.”​

The report documents observations, learning outcomes, and technical insights that bridge the gap between classroom theory and on-site practice.​

Contents of the Report
The main sections of the report include:​

Introduction to Nand Vatika Developers Pvt. Ltd. and the Nand Vatika Greens project

Site inspection of residential blocks and layout planning

Formwork and scaffolding systems used on site

Column reinforcement details and procedures

Project management practices and resource planning

Steel weight calculations for structural elements

Sunken slab concepts and applications

Concrete production using a batching plant

Conclusions and reflections from the internship experience

Key Learning Outcomes
During the internship, the following skills and insights were developed:​

Understanding of high-rise residential construction workflows

Hands-on exposure to reinforcement detailing and quality control

Awareness of construction safety practices and site rules

Ability to relate structural design concepts to field execution

Improved teamwork, communication, and professional work ethics
